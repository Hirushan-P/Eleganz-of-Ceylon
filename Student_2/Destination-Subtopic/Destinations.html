Destinations

Places to visit

Sri Lanka is offering you an island filled with lots of places to visit.
From beaches and sandy coasts to heritages and ancient cities
this country is filled with places you've never seen.
so come on and explore! Dont forget to wrap your holidays around to visit all of these.






Beaches



The beaches in Sri Lanka are a sign of heaven. They are well known for their breath-taking scenery. Beaches are all around Sri Lanka, but some beaches are a must-visit, such as Mirissa, Polhena, Unawatuna, and Hikkaduwa, which surround the southern part of the country.

Negombo beach is also another beautiful coast here. It is located in the western part of Sri Lanka. Negombo is also very famous and known for its fishing spots



Heritages



Sri Lanka's heritages are even recognized by UNESCO, which has declared 6 world heritages in the country itself. The ancient city of Anuradhapura and Polonnaruwa are two of the most famous cities.


Dambulla is also another ancient city located in matale district, mostly famous for the sigiriya rock fortress.



Nothern Sri Lanka



Northern Sri Lanka or else mainly Jaffna is a different world full of different cultures, foods and people. The jaffna fort, Nallur kovil, Jaffna public library, elephant pass are some places to visit.

The delf island a place that is rather different from all of this but unique for its beauty. A must visit of you plan on visiting the northern part of Sri Lanka.



Religious Sites



Sri Lanka is a island famous for its diversity in cultures and religions. There are many religious sites to must-visit including temples, kovils, churches and mosques. The most famous temple, dalada maligawa is located in kandy, and is the most religious site considered by the buddists in the country.

The kataragama devalaya is a place where all religions come to one place. The only temple in the world, where all the people from different religions can visit and worship.
